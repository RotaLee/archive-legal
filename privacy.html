我們收集的資料Information We Collect

Archive 可能會保存你主動輸入的帳號資訊、語言偏好、收藏名稱、照片、地點、價格、故事、標籤、願望清單、收納位置、保養任務、提醒設定與 Premium 狀態。Archive may store account details, language preference, collection names, photos, locations, prices, stories, tags, wishlist entries, storage locations, maintenance tasks, reminder settings, and Premium status that you choose to provide.

本機、免費版與 Premium 資料Local, Free, and Premium Data

免費版資料主要儲存在裝置上。若你啟用 Premium 或同步功能,部分資料(如電子郵件、收藏內容與照片)可能會透過雲端服務保存,以支援跨裝置備份、恢復與同步。Free data is primarily stored on your device. If you enable Premium or sync features, some data — such as your email, collection content, and photos — may be stored through cloud services to support backup, restore, and cross-device sync.

資料用途How We Use Information

Archive 使用資料來顯示收藏、建立貼紙與展示卡、整理地圖與月曆、提供願望清單、收納 QR Code、保養提醒、備份、同步、客服與訂閱權限。Archive 不會出售你的個人資料,也不會為了跨 App 追蹤而蒐集資料。Archive uses data to display collections, create stickers and display cards, organize maps and calendars, provide wishlists, storage QR codes, maintenance reminders, backups, sync, support, and subscription access. Archive does not sell your personal information and does not collect data for cross-app tracking.

照片與檔案Photos and Files

你加入的照片與匯出的圖片只會用於你選擇使用的功能,例如收藏展示、貼紙、分享卡與備份。若功能需要系統相機、相簿或雲端服務,Archive 只會在功能所需範圍內使用。Photos and exported images are used only for features you choose, such as collection display, stickers, share cards, and backups. When camera, photo library, or cloud services are needed, Archive uses them only as required for the feature.

通知Notifications

若你允許通知,Archive 可能會發送保養提醒、收藏相關提醒或同步狀態提醒。你可以在 iOS 系統設定中調整通知權限。If you allow notifications, Archive may send maintenance reminders, collection-related reminders, or sync status notices. You can change notification permissions in iOS Settings.

第三方服務Third-Party Services

Archive 可能使用 Apple 登入、App Store 內購,以及雲端資料庫與儲存服務(Supabase)。這些服務只會在提供 App 功能所需範圍內處理資料。Archive may use Apple Sign In, App Store in-app purchases, and cloud database and storage services (Supabase). These providers process data only as needed to deliver app features.

資料保留與刪除Retention and Deletion

你可以在 App 內「設定 → 帳號 → 刪除帳號與所有資料」自助刪除帳號,這會一併移除本機與雲端資料以及你的帳號本身。Archive 會在提供服務、備份、客服、安全或法律需求所需期間保留資料。刪除 App 或更換裝置可能會使未同步的本機資料遺失。You can delete your account yourself in the app under Settings → Account → "Delete Account & Data", which removes local and cloud data as well as the account itself. Archive keeps data as needed for service operation, backups, support, security, or legal requirements. Deleting the app or changing devices may remove local data that has not been synced.

安全Security

Archive 會採取合理的技術與管理措施保護資料,但任何 App、裝置或網路服務都無法保證絕對安全。Archive uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no app, device, or internet service can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure.

兒童與監護Children and Guardians

Archive 適合能依法使用 App 與建立帳號的使用者。未成年人使用時,應由家長或監護人了解並同意。Archive is intended for users who can lawfully use apps and create accounts. If a minor uses Archive, a parent or guardian should review and consent to use.
